— 03 / The Forge

How it gets made

Five stations between a CAD file and your shelf. None of them get skipped.

01

Design

3D modeled with panel lines and weathering points planned in.

02

Unfold

Flattened into 2D pattern sheets with Pepakura. Every face becomes a tab.

03

Print

Onto cardstock matched to the part — 250 gsm hulls, 120 gsm detail.

04

Cut & Score

Hobby knife and steel ruler. Every fold scored so paper hinges, not cracks.

05

Build & Forge

Fold, glue, paint, weather, seal. Off the bench when it passes the three-foot test.
