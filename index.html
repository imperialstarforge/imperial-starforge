About

One workshop, one desk, one printer.

Imperial Starforge is a one-person operation. Every template is designed, unfolded, and test-folded before it goes up for sale — if it doesn't fold clean, it doesn't get listed.

These are designs inspired by the sci-fi and military hardware we all grew up loving. Not officially licensed, or affiliated with any studio or manufacturer — just paper and patience.

Questions

Before you print

What paper do I need?

Plain A4 or US letter printer paper. No cardstock or special materials.

Do I need special software to open the file?

No, every template is delivered as a standard one-page PDF. Print it directly from any PDF viewer.

How long does a build take?

Varies by model — small pieces can fold in 15–30 minutes, larger builds take longer. Each listing notes rough build time.

Can I sell models I build from these templates?

These templates are for personal, non-commercial use. Reach out directly if you're interested in something beyond that.
